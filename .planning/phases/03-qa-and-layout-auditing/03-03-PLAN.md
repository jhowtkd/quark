# Plan 03-03 — Graph UI and Interaction Verification

## Scope
Verify that the Graph UI renders legibly against the new Blueprint Noir monochrome backgrounds and that graph-related components retain their styling contracts. Ensure nodes, edges, labels, and detail panels are readable and that hover/selection interactions remain visually distinct.

## Must Haves (derived from phase goal)
1. GraphPanel.vue background is `#e2e2e2` and provides enough contrast for D3-rendered nodes, edges, and text.
2. Node labels, edge labels, and detail panels use colors that remain readable on the new backgrounds.
3. Step1GraphBuild.vue cards and buttons follow the monochrome component contract.
4. Process.vue graph container and detail panel do not have layout overflows or clipped text after styling changes.
